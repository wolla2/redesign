Interested in Becoming a Member?

We welcome visitors who are interested in hearing a guest speaker or just want to see what Rotary is all about first hand. Please join us any Tuesday at the Covington Radisson for our weekly meeting. Greeting and networking begin at 12:00pm and the cost is $15. We look forward to meeting you and sharing what it means to be a Rotarian.


If you would like more information on how to become a member, contact﻿ our Membership Chair: Carol Rodgers-Rivir or co-Chair Mitchell Light.
